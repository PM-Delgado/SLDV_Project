Chega and Discrimination in Portugal

Like many other far-right political parties, Chega is known for it's minorities discrimation idiologies, specially against the romani ethnicity in Portugal.

Therefore, this project was made in order to visualize the amount of discrimation complaints reported by minorities, highlighting the romani ethnicity, taking into consideration Chega's presence in the parlament and it's rise to power.

All the data used in this project was taken from Portuguese Parlament's official website and from the Commission for Equality and Against Racial Discrimination.
